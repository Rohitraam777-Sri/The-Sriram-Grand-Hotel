About The Dish

🍛 Second Image: Dish: Kerala Parotta with Chicken Curry Details: Parotta — a soft, flaky South Indian flatbread made by folding and roasting dough layers. Paired with spicy chicken curry cooked with bell peppers, onions, and tomato gravy (often “chicken roast” style). Sliced onions on the side — a typical touch in South Indian meals. Flavor: Bold, spicy, and aromatic with coconut oil or ghee undertones. Both plates celebrate India’s love for layered parottas — one from the North with paneer and the other from the South with fiery chicken! 🇮🇳🔥
