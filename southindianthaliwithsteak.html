About The Dish

This is a Korean-style Bulgogi Bowl — a colorful and nutritious meal served in a single bowl. What’s included: 🍚 Steamed Rice as the base. 🥩 Bulgogi: Thinly sliced marinated beef cooked with soy sauce, garlic, sesame oil, and onions — tender and flavorful. 🍳 Sunny-Side-Up Egg: Adds richness and creaminess. 🥒 Cucumber Ribbons and 🥬 Kimchi: Refreshing and tangy sides that balance the rich meat. 🌿 Coriander and Spring Onions for garnish. Flavor profile: Sweet, savory, garlicky, with a touch of spice and tang — classic Korean harmony in a bowl.
