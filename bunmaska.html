About The Dish

Simple yet classic Indian tea-time combo — Bun Maska and Chai. ☕🍞 Bun Maska – a soft, slightly sweet bread bun sliced and generously layered with butter (maska). It’s fluffy inside and lightly toasted or served fresh. Cutting Chai – a small glass of strong, milky Indian tea with a perfect balance of sweetness and spice (often flavored with cardamom or ginger). This pairing is especially famous in Mumbai cafés and Irani bakeries, known for being comforting, affordable, and perfect for relaxed evening conversations.
