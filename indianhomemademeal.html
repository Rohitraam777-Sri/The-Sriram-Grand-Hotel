About The Dish

a simple and comforting Indian homemade meal (thali) — a perfect example of everyday Indian food that’s both nutritious and delicious. 🍛 Here’s what’s on the plate: 🥣 Dal Tadka: A bowl of yellow lentil curry (usually made with toor dal or moong dal) tempered with ghee, mustard seeds, garlic, and cumin for rich flavor. 🍆 Bhindi Fry: Crispy fried okra (lady’s finger) cooked with spices — slightly crunchy and flavorful. 🥬 Cabbage Peas Curry: Finely chopped cabbage and green peas sautéed with tomatoes and spices for a mildly spiced sabzi. 🍚 Steamed White Rice: Soft, fluffy basmati rice that pairs perfectly with the dal. 🫓 Chapati (Roti): Soft Indian flatbreads made from whole wheat flour, served warm. 🧅 Onion, Lemon & Green Chili: Fresh accompaniments to add tang, crunch, and a little heat. This wholesome plate represents a classic North Indian vegetarian meal — balanced with protein, fiber, carbs, and just the right amount of spice.
