About The Dish

A delicious North Indian meal platter featuring Lachha Paratha, Paneer Curry, and Rasgulla for dessert. 🍽✨ Here’s what’s on the plate: 🫓 Lachha Paratha: A flaky, layered Indian flatbread made with whole wheat flour and cooked with ghee or butter. Its crisp, spiral texture makes it perfect for scooping up curries. 🧀 Paneer Curry: Soft cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich, spicy tomato-onion gravy with aromatic masalas. Garnished with fresh coriander leaves and green chilies. 🍮 Rasgulla (in the bowl): A classic Bengali sweet made from chenna (cottage cheese balls) soaked in light sugar syrup — soft, juicy, and refreshing. This meal beautifully balances spicy, buttery, and sweet flavors, offering the complete essence of an Indian thali experience.
