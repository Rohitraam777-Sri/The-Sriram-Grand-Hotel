About The Dish

Delicious Indian thali (meal plate) consisting of: Paratha/Chapati – soft, golden-brown Indian flatbreads brushed with ghee. Paneer curry – a creamy dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a rich, spiced gravy. Chole (chickpea curry) – chickpeas cooked in a tangy tomato-based gravy. Vegetable pulao – aromatic yellow rice cooked with vegetables like carrots and beans. Sliced onions – served as a fresh salad accompaniment. It’s a complete North Indian-style meal, colorful and well-balanced with flavors of spice, richness, and freshness.
