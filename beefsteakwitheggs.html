About The Dish

Hearty steak and eggs breakfast — a classic Western-style meal that’s rich and filling. 🥩🍳 Here’s what’s on the plate: Grilled steak – perfectly seared with a golden-brown crust, seasoned simply with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Sunny-side-up eggs – fried eggs with bright yellow yolks and crisp edges, adding creaminess to the dish. Golden fried potatoes – diced and pan-fried until soft inside and crispy outside, seasoned with pepper for extra flavor. It’s a protein-packed breakfast often enjoyed for its bold flavors and satisfying balance of textures — tender steak, creamy eggs, and crispy potatoes.
