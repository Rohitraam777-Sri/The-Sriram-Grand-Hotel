About The Dish

classic Indian meal featuring Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) served with steamed basmati rice and a piece of roti (Indian flatbread). 🍛🫓 Here’s what’s on the plate: 🍗 Butter Chicken: Tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based gravy made with butter, cream, and aromatic spices like garam masala, fenugreek, and cumin. It has a smooth, mildly spiced, and buttery flavor. 🍚 Basmati Rice: Fluffy, long-grain rice served in a neat mound, perfect for soaking up the buttery sauce. 🫓 Roti/Chapati: A soft Indian flatbread that complements the curry — ideal for scooping up the flavorful sauce. 🌿 Garnish: Fresh coriander leaves sprinkled on top for color and freshness. It’s a comforting, restaurant-style North Indian dish, known for its creamy texture and balanced spice — one of the most loved Indian curries worldwide.
