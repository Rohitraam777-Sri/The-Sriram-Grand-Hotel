About The Dish

This dish is Japanese Chicken Katsu Curry, a hearty and beloved Japanese meal. What’s on the plate: 🍚 Steamed White Rice sprinkled with black sesame seeds. 🍗 Chicken Katsu: Breaded and deep-fried chicken cutlet sliced into strips — crispy outside, tender inside. 🍛 Japanese Curry: Thick, mildly spiced curry sauce made with onions, carrots, and potatoes, poured beside the rice and katsu. Flavor profile: Mildly spicy, savory, slightly sweet, and very comforting — a perfect balance of crunchy and saucy textures.
