About The Dish

Delicious egg sandwich, perfectly toasted and stuffed with a flavorful filling. 🥪🍳 Bread – golden-brown and crisp, grilled with butter for a crunchy texture. Filling – made of scrambled eggs mixed with finely chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, and mild spices, giving it a rich and savory taste. Garnish – sprinkled with fresh coriander on top for freshness and color. This is a popular Indian-style egg sandwich, often enjoyed for breakfast or an evening snack — hearty, protein-packed, and full of flavor.
