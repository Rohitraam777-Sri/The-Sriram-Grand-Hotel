About The Dish

A Traditional Indian thali (meal plate) — a wholesome combination of flavors, colors, and textures served neatly in a stainless steel tray. 🍽🇮🇳 Here’s what’s included: 🫓 Lachha Paratha: Flaky, layered whole wheat flatbread cooked with ghee, soft inside and crisp outside. 🍚 Jeera Rice: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with cumin seeds, garnished with fresh coriander and green chilies. 🍛 Dal Tadka: A comforting yellow lentil curry tempered with ghee, garlic, and Indian spices like cumin and mustard seeds. 🥘 Paneer Bhurji: Crumbled paneer cooked with onions, tomatoes, and green chilies — spicy, rich, and full of flavor. 🧅 Salad: Sliced onions and a green chili for freshness and crunch. 🍮 Gulab Jamun: A soft, syrup-soaked sweet dumpling made from khoya (milk solids), adding the perfect sweet finish. This thali represents a balanced North Indian meal — featuring carbs, protein, spice, and sweetness, all in one delicious plate.
