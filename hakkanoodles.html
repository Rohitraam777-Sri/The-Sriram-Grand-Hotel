About The Dish

Steaming hot Chicken Hakka Noodles, a classic Indo-Chinese street food favorite. 🍜🔥 🍴 What’s on the plate: Noodles: Stir-fried, long and springy, coated in soy and chili sauces. Chicken pieces: Tender, pan-seared chunks mixed into the noodles. Vegetables: Sliced bell peppers, spring onions, and bean sprouts adding crunch and color. Toppings: Freshly chopped green onions for garnish. Steam rising: Shows it’s just cooked — hot, aromatic, and full of wok flavor (“wok hei”). 🌶 Flavor Profile: Savory, smoky, and slightly spicy — with the perfect balance of soy sauce, chili, and garlic. In short, it’s a sizzling plate of Chicken Hakka Noodles, bursting with street-style flavor and freshness — pure comfort food with an irresistible aroma. .
