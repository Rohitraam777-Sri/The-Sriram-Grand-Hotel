About The Dish

This Steaming hot plate of chicken biryani — a royal and aromatic South Asian rice dish known for its rich flavors and vibrant colors. 🍗🍛 Here’s what it features: Long-grain basmati rice cooked with spices like saffron, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon, giving it a golden hue. Juicy chicken drumsticks, marinated and cooked in a spiced gravy before being layered with the rice. Fresh herbs like mint and coriander leaves sprinkled on top for aroma and garnish. Sides of lemon wedges and sliced onions, often used to enhance the flavor when serving. The dish is served in a traditional handi (metal pot), keeping it warm and fragrant — a true symbol of festive Indian and Mughlai cuisine.
