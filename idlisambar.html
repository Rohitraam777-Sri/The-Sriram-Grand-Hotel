About The Dish

Delicious serving of Idli Sambar, a classic South Indian comfort food. Idlis – soft, fluffy steamed rice cakes, light and spongy in texture. Sambar – a flavorful lentil-based curry cooked with tamarind, spices, and vegetables, poured generously over the idlis. Coconut chutney – seen on the side, made from ground coconut, roasted chana dal, green chilies, and tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves. This dish is a popular breakfast or tiffin item across South India — warm, nutritious, and full of authentic flavor.
