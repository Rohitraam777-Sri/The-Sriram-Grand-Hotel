About The Dish

This dish is Kuzhi Paniyaram (also called Appe or Paddu in some regions) 🥰✨ It’s a South Indian breakfast or snack made from idli or dosa batter, cooked in a special pan with small round moulds. Served with: 🥥 Coconut chutney 🧄 Tomato or kara chutney 🥛 Sometimes curd chutney too
