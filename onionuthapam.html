About The Dish

South Indian breakfast platter that looks absolutely delicious and colorful. It includes: Uttapam – thick, soft dosas topped with chopped onions, grated carrots, tomatoes, coriander, and green chilies. Medu Vada – crispy, golden-brown lentil fritters shaped like doughnuts, crunchy outside and fluffy inside. Chutneys – three varieties served on top: Coconut chutney (white) Green chutney (mint or coriander) Tomato or red chili chutney (orange) Sambar – a bowl of spicy and tangy lentil-based vegetable curry (not clearly visible but typically paired). Filter coffee – traditional South Indian coffee served frothy in a steel tumbler. It’s a perfect South Indian breakfast combo — hearty, flavorful, and comforting..
